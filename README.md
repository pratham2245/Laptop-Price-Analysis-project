# Laptop-Price-Analysis-project

This project focuses on analyzing laptop pricing data to understand how different hardware specifications and brands influence laptop prices.  
The analysis follows an **industry-style data analyst workflow**, emphasizing exploratory data analysis (EDA), visualization, and business insights.

---

##  Objective
The objective of this project is to:
- Analyze laptop price distribution across the market
- Identify key factors influencing laptop prices
- Compare pricing across brands and hardware configurations
- Provide insights useful for pricing strategy and customer decision-making

---

##  Dataset Description
The dataset contains information about laptops and their specifications, including:

- Brand  
- Processor Type  
- RAM  
- Storage  
- Price  

Each row represents a laptop model with its associated features and selling price.

---

##  Key Focus Areas
- Price distribution and market segmentation  
- Brand-wise price comparison  
- Impact of RAM on laptop pricing  
- Impact of storage type and capacity on price  
- Relationship between processor type and price  
- Identification of budget vs premium laptop segments  

---

##  Data Cleaning & Preparation
The following preprocessing steps were performed:
- Handling missing values  
- Converting price column to numeric format  
- Standardizing categorical variables  
- Removing duplicates and invalid records  

These steps ensure data consistency and reliable analysis.

---

##  Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Key visualizations created in this project include:
- Price distribution histogram  
- Brand-wise average price comparison  
- RAM vs Price analysis  
- Storage vs Price analysis  
- Processor type vs Price comparison  

Each visualization is designed to highlight pricing patterns and feature impact.

---

##  Key Insights
- Laptop prices vary significantly across brands  
- Higher RAM configurations generally result in higher prices  
- SSD-based laptops are priced higher than HDD-based models  
- Premium processors significantly increase laptop prices  
- The market is clearly segmented into budget and premium categories  

---

##  Business Use & Impact
The insights from this analysis can help:
- Businesses design competitive pricing strategies  
- Sellers optimize product configurations  
- Customers make informed purchasing decisions  
- Analysts understand hardware-driven pricing trends
